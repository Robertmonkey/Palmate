Location of the Duneshelter


Duneshelter
 (357, 347) is a desert town located inside a crumbling palace, protected by 
Marcus
 and his 
PIDF
 forces, Canonically, civilians make this place their home, including two 
Wandering Merchants
, and a 
Pal Merchant
