Paldium Fragment is a material in Palworld.
Acquisition
It can be obtained by:
Mining Paldium rocks.
Random drop while mining regular rocks.
A Crusher can process Stone into Paldium Fragments.
Some Pals can also drop Paldium Fragment when captured or defeated.
Use
It's primarily needed to craft Pal Spheres.
It is required to craft many structures and gear pieces.
