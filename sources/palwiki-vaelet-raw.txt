'''Vaelet''' (Japanese: '''ヴィオェッタ''' ''Violetta'') is a {{i|Grass}} [[Elements|element]] [[Pals|pal]].

== Appearance ==
Vaelet has a green and black body with purple petals on its back. The top part of its body is covered in white fur.

== Behavior and habitat ==
Its hostile and will attack on sight.
It can be found in the [[No. 1 Wildlife Sanctuary]], and as an alpha at the Sealed Realm Of The Guardian quick travel.
