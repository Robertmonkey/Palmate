Pal Metal Ingot is a material crafted by the player. It can also be dropped by select late-game Pals and is used for various items.
Pal Metal Ingots are made at the Electric Furnace with a workload of 500.
The crafting recipe consumes Ore ×4 and Paldium Fragment ×2 to output Pal Metal Ingot ×1.
