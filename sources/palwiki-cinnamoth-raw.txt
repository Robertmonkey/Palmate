'''Cinnamoth''' (Japanese: '''パピテフ''' ''Papitefu'') is a {{i|Grass}} [[Elements|element]] [[Pals|Pal]].

== Appearance ==
Cinnamoth is a small moth-like Pal with a primarily yellow body and orange-yellow wings, the tips of it's feelers and sections of it's wings have purple and white bands, while the lower wingtips of Cinnamoth's wings are a bright yellow, there is also a cream coloured hairy area around it's neck.

== Behavior and habitat ==
It typically can be seen in groups and will immediately escape upon spotting a player.
[[File:Cinnamoth_Day_Habitat.webp|thumb|Daytime Cinnamoth Spawns]]
[[File:Cinnamoth_Night_Habitat.webp|thumb|Nightime Cinnamoth Spawns]]

== Utility ==
{{PAGENAME}} is able to plant crops and craft medicine at a [[Medieval Medicine Workbench|Medieval]] or [[Electric Medicine Workbench]].

== Trivia ==
* [[Bristla]]'s Palpedia entry mentions that it only smiles when a Cinnamoth is drinking its nectar.
* Its name may come from the words ''cinnamon'' and ''moth''.
* Its Japanese name might be from “Papillon” and てふてふ (‘‘tefutefu’’), an old kana spelling for “butterfly.”
