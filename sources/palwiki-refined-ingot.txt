Refined Ingot is an intermediate crafting material used in many mid-game recipes. Refined ingots can be crafted using 2 ore and 2 Coal at an Improved Furnace.
This item unlocks with the Improved Furnace at Tier 34 with 4 Technology points.
Ore — 2
Coal — 2
Refined Ingot — 1
