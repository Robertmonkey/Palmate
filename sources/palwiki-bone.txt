'''Bone''' is a [[material]] used to make [[Cement]], [[Medical Supplies]], [[High Grade Medical Supplies|High-Grade Medical Supplies]], [[Suspicious Juice]], [[Strange Juice]], [[Mind Control Meds]], and [[Memory Wiping Medicine]].

You can obtain bone from various [[Pals]], though the most efficient method is to buy if from the [[Wandering Merchant]], for 100G each.

==Acquisition==
*It can be bought from a [[Wandering Merchant]].
*[[Sootseer]] can produce bones at the [[Ranch]] using its partner skill.
*Although infrequent, [[Vixy]] can dig up bones at the [[Ranch]] using its partner skill.
